Homemade Brownie

Description

Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust. They may also include nuts, frosting, chocolate chips, or other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Sugar
  • Cacoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Unsalted butter
  • Oil
  • Chocolate
  • Flour

How to make?

  1. Mix egg and sugar.
  2. Melt the butter with chocolate.
  3. Add the butter chocolate mixture to the eggs.
  4. Add the flour.
  5. Bake in the oven.
  6. Let the brownie rest before serving.

Bon appetit!
