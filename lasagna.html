World's Best Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of broad, flat pasta sheets layered with other ingredients and baked. Typical fillings include a meat sauce (ragù) or vegetable sauce, and cheeses like ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The dish is often topped with more cheese before baking, creating a savory, multi-layered casserole. Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna layers
  • Cheese

How to make?

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the lasagna leafs.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
